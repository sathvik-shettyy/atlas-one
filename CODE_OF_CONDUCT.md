# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to foster an open, welcoming, and harassment-free community.

## Our Standards

- Show empathy and respect.
- Offer constructive feedback.
- Focus on what benefits the project and community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking corrective action for unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues through private maintainer channels.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant v2.1.
